# Sexually dimorphic differences in angiogenesis markers are associated with brain aging trajectories in humans

Torres-Espin A, Radabaugh HL, Treiman S, Fitzsimons SS, Harvey D, Cortes D, Kummerfeld E, Elahi FM. Science Translational Medicine (2024) 16(775):eadk3118.

## Study Design

- **Cohort:** N=435 (207 female, mean age 74+/-9) from UCSF and UC Davis ADRCs
- **Validation:** N=80 from MarkVCID
- **Design:** Longitudinal with median 5 visits per participant
- **73% functionally normal at baseline (CDR=0)**
- **APOE4 carriers:** 30% overall (34.2% men vs 25.6% women)

## Biomarkers Measured

MSD V-Plex electrochemiluminescence: bFGF, VEGFA, VEGFB, VEGFC, PlGF, VEGFR1/sVEGFR1, Tie2

## Key Findings

### Two orthogonal angiogenesis components (NL-PCA)
- **PC1 "Aberrant Angiogenesis" (AA):** PlGF, VEGFR1, VEGFA, VEGFC (24.9% variance)
- **PC2 "Vascular Health" (VH):** bFGF(+), VEGFA(+), VEGFC(+) vs PlGF(-), VEGFR1(-), Tie2(-) (18.8% variance)

### Sex-dimorphic associations
- Younger women: higher AA = better executive function, higher GMV, lower WMH
- Older women (>75): higher AA = worse executive function, lower GMV, higher WMH
- Men: inverse association between AA and executive function across ages
- GMV mediates 48% of AA effect on executive function in women only (not men)

### Amyloid-independent effects
- Amyloid-negative MCI has higher AA scores than amyloid-positive MCI
- bFGF higher in cognitively normal vs MCI regardless of amyloid status

### Effect sizes
- Partial eta-squared range 0.009-0.031 for angiogenesis-brain associations
- Strongest: PlGF x Age x Sex on GMV (0.031)

## Mechanistic Hypotheses
- VEGFR1 age-related alternative splicing -> sVEGFR1 (=sFLT1) sequesters VEGFA
- PlGF-VEGFA heterodimers dampen angiogenic activation
- bFGF attenuates astrocyte activation (reduces GFAP) + neurotrophic

## Methods
- Linear mixed models with random intercept, Type III SS, alpha=0.05
- Mediation: bootstrap 1000 resamples
- Missing data: MICE m=20 imputations
- MRI: Siemens Prisma 3T, 1mm isotropic T1, FLAIR for WMH

## Limitations
- Correlative despite causal modeling tests
- Limited cohort diversity
- Possible underpowering for smaller effects
- MSD V-Plex measures both sVEGFR1 and membrane-bound VEGFR1 without distinguishing isoforms
